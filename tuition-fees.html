Frequently Asked Questions

Can I work while studying in Morocco?
Yes, student visa allows 20 hours/week part-time work. Common jobs: English tutoring $8-15/hour, tourism $6-10/hour, freelancing online. No work permit needed.
Are there scholarships for international students?
AMCI Scholarship: Full tuition + $200/month + free dorm. Islamic Development Bank: Full funding for STEM. University merit scholarships: 25-100% tuition reduction. Apply March-June via Moroccan embassy.
Is Morocco safe for international students?
Morocco ranks safest in North Africa. Global Peace Index: Better than USA, Brazil, South Africa. 50,000+ international students. Female-friendly with dedicated university support. 24/7 campus security.
