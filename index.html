Tips & Guidance

Applications

Start 12-18 months early. Tailor your personal statement. Get strong recommendation letters from teachers.

Scholarships

Research merit-based and need-based scholarships. Check Fulbright, Chevening, DAAD, and Moroccan government programs.

Documents

Prepare: passport, transcripts, diplomas, language certificates, financial statements, motivation letter.

Interviews

Practice common questions. Show passion for your field. Research the university and program thoroughly.
