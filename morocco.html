2026 Admission Requirements - Public Schools

Universities (UH2C/UM5): Bac any type. No concours for Licence. Registration July 15 - Sept 15. Master: Licence + concours + interview.

Engineering (EHTP/EMI/ENSEM): Bac Sciences Mathématiques mention Bien/Très Bien. CNC National Concours mandatory. Registration Feb-Mar 2026.

ENCG: Bac + TAFEM National Concours. Registration Feb-Apr 2026. 5-year integrated Master.

OFPPT/CMC: Bac or Niveau Bac for TS. No concours. Selection by grades. Registration May-July 2026 via takwine.ofppt.ma

Medical Schools: Bac Sciences Expérimentales/Physiques mention Bien minimum. National concours July 2026.
